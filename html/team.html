Our Team

SPIC MACAY, the Society for Promotion of Indian Classical Music And Culture Amongst Youth, a voluntary and non-profit movement,was started in 1977 by Dr. Kiran Seth to promote Indian Culture amongst youth.

The IIT Roorkee chapter of SPIC MACAY, started in 1985, has been working relentlessly to uphold the spirit and zeal of the movement . A large number of diverse events have been held in the past to give the students and community a feeling of our rich cultural heritage. SPIC MACAY is completely managed by a group of student enthusiasts,right from preparing the venue, providing a befitting hospitality to the artist and raising funds through donations and sponsorships from individuals and corporate houses. The IIT Roorkee Chapter organizes a number of programs on campus as well as in the neighboring schools and COER(College of Engineering,Roorkee).
